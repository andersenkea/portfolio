Kate Andersen is a Tampa Bay-based designer with a dynamic background in branding, motion, digital, web, and exhibition design. Since launching her career in 2022, Kate has focused on crafting thoughtful, impact-driven work for environmental nonprofits and earth-conscious brands.


Her designs are inspired by a passion for modern design and the natural sciences, fostering collaborations that bridge these fields. Beyond her professional practice, Kate has begun to explore the potential of bio-materials, transforming food waste and other biodegradable resources into sustainable product prototypes.
